Window Cleaning Services

Clean windows transform the appearance of any living area or work space to look more immaculate and cheery. Airborne contaminants, rainstorms, and hard water mineralization can cause your windows to look dingy, and your rooms to seem duller. At EnviroKleen, we offer comprehensive window cleaning services to brighten your rooms. With a glimmering, clear surface, your facilities windows will let in the rejuvenating light that both your customers and employees need.

EnviroKleen’s extensive range of window cleaning services brings about the best shine for your windows:

  • Exterior squeegee cleaning (from single storied buildings, to high-rises)
  • Screen cleaning
  • Hard water stain removal
  • Interior partition/interior glass surfaces
  • Pressure washing
  • Skylight cleaning
  • And more!

When the experienced cleaners at EnviroKleen manage your windows, the edges and sills are wiped clean and dry, leaving nothing but sparkling glass. EnviroKleen emphasizes the safety of our employees; each member of our team receives thorough training to ensure that both they and your facility are safe from harm—no matter how many stories up they are! Our team also knows to respect your employees, cleaning only during appropriate times so as to not distract your clients.

Get started with EnviroKleen today! With our comprehensive services, we can manage all levels of commercial janitorial work, including your carpet & floors, grounds work, windows, and more! Whether you would like your windows washed regularly, or a single emergency cleaning, EnviroKleen’s professional staff can handle the job, giving your building an inviting ambiance to help attract business.
